Money 4 MV

What is Money 4 MV?

Money 4 MV is a club at Monte Vista High School in Danville CA, started in 2017 by Daniel Kao

Purpose

This club was founded with the purpose of making Monte Vista High School more eco friendly. Separate bins are placed around the school to collect bottles and cans separate from trash and are collected periodically by the club members. The bottles and cans are then sold at a local recycling facility and the money is then donated back to the school.
